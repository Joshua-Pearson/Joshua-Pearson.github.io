About Me

4 years ago, I moved out to San Francisco where my love for technology blossomed. I graduated from WDI, a Web Development Immersive program offered by General Assembly in June, 2014. In four months I accumulated over 400+ hours of instruction and spent over 600+ hours coding. I developed proficiencies in Javascript, Angular.js, Node.js, Rails, Ruby, Coffeescript, Meteor and more.

I have a background in customer service, criminal justice and philosophy which honed my problem-solving and critical thinking skills, which I combine with my passion for programming and technology. I am constantly on the lookout for like-minded individuals to learn from, to collaborate with, and help develop amazing products and ideas.

When I am not on my laptop coding or programming, you can find me at the beach, playing racquetball or hovering over a chess board in the park. Feel free to contact me through email, twitter or facebook. You can also check out my LinkedIn profile or Github. Cheers!
