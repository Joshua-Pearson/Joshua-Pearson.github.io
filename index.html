About Me

4 years ago, I turned down law school and moved out to San Francisco where my love for technology blossomed. I graduated from WDI, a Web Development Immersive program in June, 2014. In four months I accumulated over 400+ hours of instruction and spent over 600+ hours coding. I developed proficiencies in Javascript, Angular, node.js, Rails, Ruby, Coffeescript, Meteor and more.

I have a background in customer service for the past 5 years, before finding my love of programming, which honed my problem-solving and critical thinking skills which I combine with my passion for programming and technology. I am always looking for like-minded individuals where to learn from, to collaborate, and help develop amazing products and ideas.

When I am not on my laptop coding or programming, I love going to the beach, playing racquetball or playing some chess in the park over a beer. Feel free to contact me through email, twitter or facebook. You can also check out my LinkedIn profile or Github.
